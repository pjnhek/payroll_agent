CRITICAL on hours nullability (Finding #3, per D-05): hours fields MUST be `Decimal | None`, not plain `Decimal`. When an extraction LLM cannot parse a particular hours field from the email, it returns null/None for that field. If hours fields were non-nullable, an email with missing hours would raise a Pydantic ValidationError before decide() can inspect the missing values, populate missing_fields, and gate the run to clarification — turning a clarification case into an unhandled parse crash. The nullability is specifically what lets the deterministic gate (LLM-07/D-08) see the gap. This does NOT relax D-05 (hours remain Decimal not float); it makes them optional. Phase 2's decide() checks `e.hours_regular is None` to populate missing_fields.
