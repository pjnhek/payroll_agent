**Fixed at the root (commit 6b6516b):** A new STEP 2.5 in the Round-2 block of `resume_pipeline` (`app/pipeline/orchestrator.py:487-532`) reconciles the paid value from the reply before `_run_stages` sees `raw_extracted`.
