<objective>
Move authority to the durable handoff and make the actual Resend boundary reject an authorization that expires after handler authorization but before external I/O.

Purpose: an earlier handler-only deadline check left attachment/snapshot preparation outside the safety calculation. The pinned synchronous `resend==2.32.2` SDK sends through its process-global `resend.default_http_client`, whose default implementation is `resend.RequestsClient` and forwards its constructor timeout to `requests.request`; timeout control must therefore be static and concurrency-safe, not an `httpx` or per-call global mutation.
Output: one exact authorization-to-provider contract, bounded PII-safe outcomes, and a clock-controlled gateway proof that expired authorization makes no Resend request.
</objective>
