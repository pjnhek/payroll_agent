Refactor confirmation approval into one-time snapshot creation followed by durable send scheduling.

Artifacts this phase produces: one-time confirmation snapshot builder and atomic approval-to-send-job handoff. The confirmation producer stops calling the legacy caller-argument gateway method here, but Plan 12 keeps compatibility removal gated on Plan 10 migrating clarification too.
