<objective>
Give ambiguous clarification delivery the operator workflow that confirmation delivery already has, without turning a delivery question into alias confirmation.

Purpose: a question that may have reached the client must show the exact frozen question and offer only safe, purpose-specific choices; the operator must never be pushed into resolving names merely because the delivery attempt was ambiguous.
Output: purpose-aware review loading, frozen-question evidence, retry/handled/reject actions, generic-recovery guards, and dashboard regressions.
</objective>

## Artifacts this phase produces

- A route projection that distinguishes confirmation from clarification/field-regression review and serves the persisted frozen question only.
- Three clarification actions: retry the same frozen question through the existing durable row while still eligible, mark handled without another email, or reject without alias writes.
- A server-rendered card and style treatment that keeps clarification review separate from the generic needs-operator name-resolution card.
- Automated UI/controller evidence plus an end-of-phase browser check; skipped live database evidence remains unavailable, not green.
