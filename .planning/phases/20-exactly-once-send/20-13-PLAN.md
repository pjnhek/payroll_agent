<objective>
Close the core exactly-once settlement holes where an ambiguous provider outcome could otherwise become an unreviewed duplicate.

Purpose: every SEND_OUTBOUND outcome, including a worker crash after provider acceptance, must retain the original frozen reservation and make the next action explicit and purpose-aware.
Output: persisted logical-email fencing, strict replay classification, safe final-lease reaping, one lock order, and falsifiable crash/concurrency regressions.
</objective>

## Artifacts this phase produces

- A delivery settlement path that compares the claimed email identity with the persisted job row before any reservation or attempt write.
- A final-lease branch for confirmation and clarification that appends bounded ambiguity evidence and enters the matching delivery-review state.
- An explicit replay-reason allowlist and same-row clarification operator-retry seam.
- Hermetic and guarded live-Postgres proofs; a skipped live proof is reported as unavailable evidence, never as a pass.
