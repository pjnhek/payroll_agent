<objective>
Promote the existing kill-worker-mid-run durability proof in place (D-01: do not rewrite a
working proof, do not duplicate it into a new canonical file — two copies drift apart silently),
give it its PROOF-01 identity (D-02), and close the falsification half of ROADMAP criterion 1 by

test, with the lease-reclaim clause **or the attempts-increment** removed, demonstrably goes red,
with a pasted red run proving the proof is not vacuous. RESEARCH.md Finding 1 verifies the
incumbent test is complete and non-vacuous, so the remaining work is identity plus executed
falsification.

Output: the PROOF-01 marker on the audited incumbent, and a SUMMARY carrying the executed
mutation evidence that `docs/DURABILITY-PROOFS.md` (plan 21-11) will publish.

**Mutation target assignment.** ROADMAP criterion 1 permits either the lease-reclaim clause or
the attempts-increment. This plan takes the **attempts-increment**; PROOF-04 (plan 21-08) takes
the expired-lease reclaim clause, which its own criterion 4 names explicitly. Two proofs sharing
one mutation target would mean one of them was never independently falsified.
</objective>
